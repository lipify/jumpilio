Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Jumpilio ("the Game", "we", "us") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Jumpilio does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game data (scores, achievements, settings, coins) is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences and is never sent to any server.

2. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads in the Game. The Game shows two types of ads:

  • Interstitial ads — full-screen ads shown periodically between games
  • Rewarded video ads — optional ads you can watch to earn in-game rewards (coin multipliers, revives)

Jumpilio is a family-friendly game suitable for all ages. All ads are limited to family-safe content (PG-rated or below).

AdMob may collect data to serve ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (Android Ad ID / IDFA)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • General location (IP-based, not precise GPS)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy and Google Ads Policy. We do not have access to this data.

3. Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob — advertising (AdMob Privacy)
  • Google Play Services — app distribution and updates

4. Children's Privacy

Jumpilio is designed to be safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The Game:

  • Does not collect, store, or transmit personal data
  • Restricts all ads to family-safe content (PG-rated or below)
  • Contains no user accounts, social features, or chat functionality

If you are a parent or guardian and have any concerns, please contact us.

5. Data Storage and Security

All game progress (scores, achievements, statistics, coins, settings, purchased skins) is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. We do not operate any servers or databases that store user data. Uninstalling the Game will permanently delete all locally stored data.

The Game does not require an internet connection to play. Internet is only used to load and display advertisements.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data for us to provide, modify, or delete. You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID / Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance.
